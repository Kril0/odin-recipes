Bourbon chicken is a dish with New Orleans roots that's easy to make with chicken thigh pieces cooked in a sweet brown sugar, bourbon, ginger, garlic, and soy sauce glaze. Serve with rice for a restaurant-worthy meal.

I love bouron chicken, it empowers me and all my ancestors

  • Bourbon
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce glaze
  • Rice
  • CornStartch
  • Vineger
  • Red Pepper
  • Green onions
  1. Toss the chicken in the cornstarch
  2. Cook the chicken in oil, then transfer it to a plate using a slotted spoon
  3. Cook the onion, garlic, and giner in oil in the same skillet
  4. In a bowl, mix the cornstarch, sauce, and broth, bourbon, sugar, vinegar, and red pepper
  5. Add the sauce mixture to the skillet, then cook and stir until thickened and bubbly
  6. Return chicken to skillet, cook, and stir until heated through
  7. Serve over rice and top with green onions
